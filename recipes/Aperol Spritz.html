Apreol Spritz

This refreshing Aperol spritz recipe is as gorgeous as it is beautiful. It’s the perfect way to unwind on a hot summer day.

Ingredients

  • Ice: Serve the Aperol spritz over ice.
  • Prosecco: You don’t have to use a top shelf Prosecco for this cocktail, but make sure your bottle is good quality.
  • Club soda: It wouldn’t be a “spritz” without bubbly club soda.
  • Aperol: Of course, you’ll need Aperol! You can use another bitter orange aperitif, if you like.
  • Orange: Garnish each Aperol spritz with an orange slice.

How to make Aperol Spritz?

Fill a wine glass with ice cubes; pour in Prosecco and club soda. Top cocktail with Aperol; garnish with orange slice.
